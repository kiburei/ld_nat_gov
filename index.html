Ministry of lands And Physical Planning

The Ministry of Lands and Physical Planning is a central player in Kenya’s socio-economic development plan. Land is one of the key enablers of the ‘Big 4 Agenda’ and the Vision 2030. Land is acknowledged as a key determinant of social, economic and political transformation.

Our Mission

To facilitate improvement of the livelihood of Kenyans through efficient land administration, equitable access, secure tenure and sustainable management of land resource

OUR VISION

To be a globally competitive organization in sustainable land management
